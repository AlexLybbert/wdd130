Our Rafting Trips

Choose from our selection of thrilling white water rafting adventures

Gentle Rapids Adventure

Difficulty: Beginner

Duration: 3 hours

Perfect for families and first-time rafters! Experience the beauty of Utah's rivers with Class I and II rapids. Our expert guides will teach you the basics while you enjoy stunning canyon views and refreshing water. This calm journey is ideal for building confidence and creating wonderful memories.

Price: $45 per person

Adrenaline Rush Rapids

Difficulty: Intermediate

Duration: 5 hours

For those seeking more excitement! Navigate through Class III and IV rapids with heart-pounding action and technical challenges. Our experienced guides will help you master advanced paddling techniques while you experience the raw power of whitewater. Includes lunch and all equipment. A thrilling half-day adventure you won't forget!

Price: $75 per person

Extreme Canyon Expedition

Difficulty: Advanced

Duration: Full Day (8 hours)

Only for experienced rafters! Conquer the most challenging Class IV and V rapids Utah has to offer. This full-day expedition takes you through breathtaking canyons with intense whitewater sections and incredible wilderness scenery. Expert guides provide instruction, and all meals are included. This is the ultimate white water rafting experience for adrenaline junkies!

Price: $120 per person
